About

We are the the University of Toronto's
annual hackathon.

/ˈhakəθɒn/: An event, typically lasting several days, in which a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative computer programming.

We look past this traditional definition of a hackathon and bring it to new heights. At UofTHacks, we bring people together to make new friends, learn new things, and bring new ideas to life.

Why Attend?

To try new things, meet new people, and to
take your skills to higher summits.

Join us and 499 other brilliant developers, creative designers, and innovative creators for 36 hours of hacking.

With awesome hardware, APIs, and assistance provided to you by our sponsors, you will have the opportunity to build something you never thought was possible.

All it takes is the willingness to try something new.

You don't have to be a pro to attend. If this will be your first hackathon, we want you to expose you to the world of creation.
